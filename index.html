To Helen

"To Helen" is a short film that revitalizes Edgar Allan Poe's poem through vintage footage from the Lavelle Home Movie Collection found on the Internet Archive. The film merges fragments of the past, inviting viewers on a journey through time and memory. This film further captures the essence of longing and remembrance through a strangers memories.

Women in 50s - 70s Ads

"The Portrayal of Women: 1950s-1970s Advertisements" is a short film that critiques the depiction of women in television ads during this pivotal era. Through found footage on the Internet Archive, the film exposes gender stereotypes and limiting roles imposed on women by these advertisements. "The Portrayal of Women" serves as a reminder of the progress made in challenging gender norms, while also highlighting the ongoing need for accurate and empowering representations of women in media.
